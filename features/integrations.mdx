### Slack Notifications (New\!)

With **automated Slack notifications**, your team can stay aligned and avoid surprises when colleagues are on leave.

### **How to Enable Slack Notifications**

1. Go to the **Integrations** page in Birdie.
2. Under **Slack**, click on **Details**.
3. Click **New Notification** and configure:
   - **Frequency**: Choose **daily, weekly, or monthly** notifications.
   - **Day & Time**: Set when the notification should be sent.
   - **Slack Channel**: Select where the notification will be posted.
4. Click **Create**, and you're all set\!
